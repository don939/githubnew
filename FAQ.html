Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a poem?
 You can send us your poem to our email address and your poem will be
uploaded on the the site within 24 hours.

What are these emotion/reactions to poems?
 You can privately share if a poem made you... smile, laugh, cry, drool, or any of the 16 reactions. This has two benefits
    You can go back to browse the poems that made you feel a certain way When enough other people also react in a similar way to a poem, that poem will be added to the "Make me... ___" page. Then anyone who is in need of a laugh, or a cry, can visit that page and find something that might give them what they're looking for.
It's an experiment, for sure, so we'd love feedback on this feature!
